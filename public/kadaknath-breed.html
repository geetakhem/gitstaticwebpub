What is Kadaknath?

As per NBAGR there are 19 breeds of native chickens in India; kadaknath is one among them. Its native to the state of Madhya Pradesh and the tribes of Bhil and Bhilala communities reared them for centuries. The specialty of the breed is the flesh and internal organs of this chicken are black in color. So it is also called as Black Meat Chicken (BMC). Its also to be noted that it is the only black meat chicken breed of India. Though the meat is black in color kadaknath breed comes in three differently coloured feathers viz. Jet black, golden and penciled.

Kadaknath is a hardy breed so it is adaptable to all climatic conditions and it has high resistance to disease.
