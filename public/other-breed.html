Other Breeds

KB Desi Chicken (Natti /Country)

KB Desi chicken is a multi-color dual purpose bird. Our company developed it by selective breeding of local Desi chickens highlighting the characters like, good egg production, colorful feathers, yellow legs, good weight gain etc. They can produce up to 180 eggs per annum and reaches 1.5kg body weight at in four months under semi-intensive farming.

Country Aseel (Commercial)

We provide Country aseel chicks (Commercial) that have good acceptability by the farmers. These chicks are in good demand for its good livability and high weight gain. Aseel hens of are matted with aseel well-built aseel fighter cocks to give this wonderful off spring. This combination produces chicks that grows with majestic look and attains body weight of 1.5kg in 90 days.

Country Aseel (Commercial)

Aseel Knife Fighter Chicks

Aseel fighter chickens are of two categories, one fights with bare legs and the in other breed a small knife is tied to the spur of the cock. We have good collection of aseel knife fighting champions and the chicks are of outstanding quality. They come up to 23-24 inches height. It’s grown for pride in rural parts of the country

Aseel Knife Fighter Chicks

Aseel Barefoot Fighter

These bare foot fighter are strong birds when compared to the knife fighters. They fight with the help of their spur. Their feathers are loose and some parts of the body are exposed. They have gigantic look and a proud gait.

Giriraja

It’s a cross breed variety of chicken developed by Karnataka government institutions. It’s a multicolor bird and good for both meat & egg production purpose. Well suited for back yard farming and scavenging conditions.

Giriraja

Guinea Fowl

Guinea fowl is a wild bird domesticated in later times of civilization. Though it originated from the African continent in the nineteenth century commercial farming of guinea fowl became popular in many parts of the world, like Asia, Europe USA etc. It feeds on the insects in the agri farms so it is used for controlling the pest. Their largely found in grey color with white dots, but also exist in white and lavender colour.

Guinea Fowl

White Pekin Duckling

White pekin is a fast growing duck breed, so it used for meat production in many countries. It has soft and tender meat and attains body weight of 2.5 kg in 42days. It consumes around 5.8kgs of feed to reach the said body weight.

White Pekin Duckling

Country Duckling

Country Duck rearing is a traditional in India. There are both nomadic duck keepers and farm based duck keepers. Both rely on the nearby water bodies or harvested fields to raise their ducks. They have indigenous breed of ducks that are suitable for their local climatic conditions. Some of the local duck breeds are Indian runner, Kutta-nadan, etc.

Country Duckling

Khaki Campbell Duckling

Khaki Campbell is a good egg producer and as denoted by its name its khaki in colour. It originated from United Kingdom and now reared in many countries for eggs. It consumes less quantity of feed and produces around 300eggs per year. It’s a good scavenger too so it’s viable for the farmer to keep it for egg production.

Khaki Campbell Duckling

Japanese Quail

Quail farming became a popular business in India after the introduction of Japanese quails. It’s a profitable venture as quail meat has good demand in the market. It’s a short term business, as the quails attain marketing weight of 200grams in 28days. In a given space a farmer can complete more number of batches per year when compared to chicken farming. It can also be used for egg production.

Japanese Quail

Turkey Bird

Turkey is a bigger poultry breed and it’s considered to be bird of festival season. In USA turkey is mostly consumed during “Thanks Giving day”, in India turkey has good demand during Ramzan, Christmas and New Year seasons. Turkey normally weighs around 5-8kgs based on its breed and gender.
